Vacine SMS Appointmnet & Reminder Confirmation

Programed a Tkinter based GUI vaccination/clinet portal with username login & password. Used a GUI based from to extract patient information (name, adress, phone number etc.). This created a vaccination appointmnet date for the 1st dosage and a second appointment date for the second dosage 3 months latter. User got an instang SMS confiming their credentials and the time and date for the first and second appointment. Patient also got an SMS notfication 1 week before their second vaccination appoiontment as a reminder. SMS fucntibnality was provided by the Twilio API.

Login/Registration Page

The user is given the option to login to their account and fill out their vaccination form. If the patinet was not registred they had the option to register with their chosen username and password.
